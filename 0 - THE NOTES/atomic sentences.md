# atomic sentences


**Atomic sentences** are also called **atoms**. They are your 'bare-bones' definition for variables in propositional logic.

Examples:
- *p*
- *q*
- *gold*
- *breeze*

The last two examples come from the [[wumpus world]] logic example.
